Refund Policy

Introduction

At Shivexa, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are available for physical and digital books purchased through our platform.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Physical books are eligible for refunds if returned within 14 days of delivery, provided they are unused and in original condition.
  • Digital books (e-books) are non-refundable unless there is a technical issue preventing access.
  • Refunds are not applicable for promotional or discounted items unless defective.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@shivexa.com with your order details.
  2. For physical books, return the item to the provided address with proof of purchase.
  3. Our team will review your request and notify you of the refund status within 5-7 business days.

Timeframe

Refunds for eligible physical books are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item. For digital book issues, refunds (if approved) are issued within 3-5 business days. Funds will be credited to your original payment method.
